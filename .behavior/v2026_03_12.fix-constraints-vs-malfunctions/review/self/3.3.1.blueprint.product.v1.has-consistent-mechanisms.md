
**why not:**
- breaks `instanceof ConstraintError` checks
- loses static properties (code, emoji)
- inconsistent with extant class-based pattern
- violates wish's implied intent

**why not:**
- breaks `instanceof BadRequestError` checks
- wish explicitly says "extend BadRequestError"
- more complex than inheritance
- inconsistent with extant pattern

**why not:**
